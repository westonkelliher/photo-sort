# Handoff — list and earn

Everything is in `~/projects/photo-sort/`. The shippable artifact is
`photo-sort-v1.zip` (8.7 KB).

## Steps (≈15 min)

1. **Sign up at gumroad.com** with your email. Add a payout method
   (PayPal or bank).
2. **New product → Digital product → Call it `photo-sort`.**
3. **Upload** `~/projects/photo-sort/photo-sort-v1.zip`.
4. **Paste the listing copy** from `GUMROAD_LISTING.md` into the description.
   Set price to **$9**.
5. **Cover image:** I described one in `GUMROAD_LISTING.md`. Use Canva (free)
   or ask me to generate an SVG.
6. **Publish.** Gumroad gives you a `gum.co/...` URL.
7. **Drive traffic** (cheapest first):
   - Reddit: `r/DataHoarder`, `r/photography`, `r/macapps`, `r/commandline`
     — write a "I built this because I had 40k photos" post, link in comments
   - Hacker News "Show HN: photo-sort — organize 10 years of phone photos in 30 sec"
   - X/Twitter with a 20-second screen recording
   - ProductHunt (Tuesday launch is best)
8. **Iterate:** If sales come from one channel, double down. If you get the
   same support question twice, fix the README.

## Honest expectations

Cold launches usually do $0–$200 in week one. With one Reddit post that hits
and a HN appearance you can do $500–$2k. The product is real and the pain is
real; the bottleneck will be reach, not quality.
